Amanda Mar is an American R&B/Pop performer and song writer from Santa Barbara, Ca. Her debut album available now! Find it on: ITunes, CD Baby, Amazon Prime, Spotify, Napster, Apple Music etc. She has just released 2 music videos documenting for her first released singles for YouTube.

 

 

 

In addition to singing with music legends Stevie Wonder and local humanitarian Kenny Loggins. Amanda has been featured singing the Star Spangled Banner for the Santa Barbara Polo & Racket Club and featured in their magazine. In addition to singing the same American tribute tune for UCSB soccer games and Santa Barbara roller derby games. Amanda Mar led and wrote lead vocal lyrics and melody for her first 3 piece band “La Radia” in 2013. La Radia performed around for a year before dispersing in early 2014.

 

 

 

Early years: Amanda has been singing and keeping rhythm since she could make sound and move. Her mom remembers her dancing to the beat of the washing machine at age 4. At the age of 6 ½ Amanda's grandmother taught her and her older sister Kristin basic harmony while she played the organ. In high school she taught herself to sing, belting out Mariah Carey and Whitney Houston to pass the time, then and there her mother Adria felt her daughter had something! After high school she sang backup harmony and occasional lead for several bands including the Anthony Prieto. This was arounf the time Amanda met producer and co-writer of debut album ( Carlos Guillen of La Cima Music Group)

 

 

 

Her mom and others encouraged her to join choir where she and her teacher found that singing soprano was her gift. This started her formal training developing her large range. After high school she sang backup harmony and occasional lead for several bands including the Anthony Prieto. This was around the time Amanda met producer and co-writer of debut album ( Carlos Guillen of La Cima Music Group)

 

 

 

Amanda went on to the Hollywood Pop Academy training in advanced vocal training, stage performance, song writing and hip-hop dance. Which led to auditions to "The Voice", America's Got Talent and American Idol. Amanda has an extensive musical resume, including working with the M.I.'s gospel choir with singers including touring musical director for Ariana Grande “Troy Laureta” and "The Voice" contestant Cheesa!! .

 

 

 

Later, in 2008 and 2011, the M.I. gospel choir was invited to sing for Stevie Wonder's House Full of Toys concert. Amanda Mar was chosen for a solo part both concerts they did with Stevie. She took up Jazz improv, beginning piano and fundamentals of music at SBCC, this is what started her journey as an independent singer, band leader and entrepreneur.

 

 

 

In 2013 she approached her friend Carlos again wanting to record a legit album with the writings as her life and experiences as the content. This is where the fun began!!!
